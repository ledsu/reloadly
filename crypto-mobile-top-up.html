How Crypto Mobile Top Ups Are Being Used By Innovative Businesses Today

Crypto Marketplaces

As cryptocurrencies surge in adoption, more businesses are providing customers with e-commerce options to trade their tokens for goods and services. Linking the crypto economy to the e-commerce economy has provided an opportunity for crypto marketplaces to integrate options to buy telecom airtime and data top ups as part of their growing catalog of products. Using an airtime API to provide this service provides easy management and global access for crypto fans.

Convenient Crypto to Mobile Top Up Exchange

In many parts of the world, mobile airtime and data wallets are commonly used as mediums for the storage of value, especially in emerging markets in Latin America, Africa and Asia. While cryptocurrencies remain illiquid in many of these regions, airtime is an easily tradable and transferable commodity. Providing customers with the ability to easily trade their illiquid bitcoin for credit in their mobile phone wallets gives them an option to turn crypto into cash faster.

Purchase of Digital Goods

Besides using bitcoin to purchase mobile airtime and data top ups via crypto economy marketplaces, an airtime API can also be used as a conduit to enable the purchase of digital goods, such as popular retailer gift cards. Through the Reloadly platform, customers can trade their bitcoin for credit to use for shopping for gift cards from Amazon, Apple and other popular brands. This means your crypto fans get access to the world of airtime top ups and so much more in just one easy integration.
