Hi, We're Reloadly

Reloadly powers the future of cross border digital mobile payments to emerging markets. Enabling the transfer of airtime, mobile money, and digital goods to be embedded into web, desktop, and mobile software apps.

BREAKING THE MOLD

As is often the case with game-changing companies, Reloadly was born out of frustration. The co-founders Michel Francis and Emmanuel Piard created the platform after actually having dealt with many traditional airtime companies for more than 5 years and seeing an opportunity to improve cumbersome mobile airtime transfer experience not just for businesses, but also for web developers and customers. Reloadly is a b2b cloud API platform for mobile top ups that lets business owners, developers and other users to setup an account online and start sending mobile top up airtime around the world in as little as 30 minutes. With a process as simple as writing few lines of codes or using our portal directly; essentially, cutting down a traditionally 3 to 6 months process down to minutes and saving businesses and developers resources, time and money.

CUTTING THE RED TAPE

To fully understand how freeing Reloadly is for business owners & web developers, consider how limiting and cumbersome getting access to traditional airtime companies currently is. One has to sign over-complicated contracts, coordinate tech teams to setup inter-connections, review lengthy files documentations of unclear APIs, which are often not well designed or build using outdated and unreliable technologies. This often leads to new or more infrastructure design and/or planning by the consumers of these APIs. Further, these platform or APIs are more than often counter intuitive and don’t provide full set of features and capabilities that businesses and developers need and want. This translate directly into them dictating what businesses or developers can do or build.

USER EXPERIENCE

Reloadly is easier to setup and use than other traditional player in the same space. We accomplish that by eliminating the friction of starting up by cutting out the bureaucracy and making the process, from start to finish, a completely self served and very intuitive one. In a matter of minutes, a user can sign up and readily have access to all the tools & credentials needed to start integration or use our portal directly. Reloadly technology is designed with developers in mind. It is a foundation that other developers can build upon, we remove the heavy lifting and allow developers and businesses to focus on what they do best. Reloadly technology does not dictate how users user or what they do with our API. With our modern approach and tooling, developers and businesses can integrate, build on top, or use our API in whatever way that suits them best. In many ways, Reloadly is like a giant lock into which developers and business can insert their own custom “key”, unlocking possibilities that allow them to fulfill other business requirements. At Reloadly, the technology underneath our hood is what sets us apart from the rest of the industry. We’re using emerging technologies and industry-wide open standards to constantly innovate while continuing to deliver the secure, reliable global service our customers rely on, 24x7. And as the pace of tech innovation rapidly increases, we know how important it is that we build a platform for the future, which is just what we’re doing. Reloadly offers a a clean, user-friendly and well documented REST API. Reloadly’s API is easy to integrate with a website, mobile app or backend system. Reloadly’s API and documentation saves users’ engineering time, at the time of launch and maintenance of integrations over time. Reloadly offers amazing developer experience through an interactive “Try it” feature which allows developers to test the API in real-time, with code examples in more than 15 different languages, right on the API documentation.
