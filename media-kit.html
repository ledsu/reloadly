Our mission

Reloadly was founded to make it easier to directly connect with global telcos. We find that current airtime APIs on the market are too bulky, so we created lightweight technology and the worldâ€™s first top-up developer toolkit that enables our users to simply integrate our API to mobile apps and websites.

Michel Francis is the CEO and Co-founder of Reloadly, which he created alongside Co-Founder Emmauel Piard in Miami, Florida in late 2019.
