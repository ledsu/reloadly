About Nigeria Telecoms

Nigeria makes up the largest mobile market share in Africa with 184 million subscribers in December 2019. National Bureau of Statistics show that the sector contributed 7.5% or approximately US$6,547.16m to the Nigerian economy. Nigerian mobile operators use 100% GSM technology for their networks. Mobile connects more Nigerians to the internet than any other technology, and has become the primary platform for creating, distributing and consuming digital content and services across multiple sectors.

There are presently four global system for mobile communications operators in Nigeria. The biggest operators in terms of market share are:

  • ⦿ MTN Nigeria, which has a 36% market share;
  • ⦿ Globacom, which has a 26% share;
  • ⦿ Airtel Nigeria, which has a 24% share; and
  • ⦿ 9mobile (formerly Etisalat Nigeria), which has a 12% share.
