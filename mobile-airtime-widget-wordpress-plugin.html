Top 6 Reasons Why Website Owners Love Reloadly’s Airtime Widget and WordPress Plugin

Not developers? No problem. Launch an airtime service without any coding

The Reloadly Airtime Widget or WordPress Plugin can be easily set up within just a few minutes, without any code writing. Using your free Reloadly account, you can easily set up the widget to generate an html code to enter into your CMS of choice. For WordPress, our widget can be downloaded here.

Integrate payments with Stripe. Manage over 100 currencies in one dashboard

Through the Reloadly dashboard, you can seamlessly integrate with Stripe to manage payments. Our dashboard provides transparent FX rates for over 100 currencies, making it easy for you to manage the payments pipeline that best suits your region and without any doubts of exchange rates.

At the moment, the Reloadly widget only integrates with Stripe. However, other payment methods will be added shortly, including Paystack, PayPal and Flutterwave.

No design skills required. Set your brand colors easily.

We provide an option to customize the Reloadly Airtime Widget with your brand colors. This allows your airtime service to act as an on-brand experience within your website. And you don’t have to be a designer to set this up.

Tap Reloadly’s global telco network. Including over 800 mobile operators.

Setting up the Reloadly Airtime Widget instantly connects you to the same global mobile operator network that users of our API benefit from. This includes over 800 operators in over 140 countries, and counting. Our widget will automatically offer a list of operators to choose from depending on the country code entered.

Easily display pricing packages. Pass on operator discounts.

Offer the pricing packages that match the recipient’s destination. There are two types of pricing rates that can be displayed, and this depends on the type of pricing packages that the selected mobile operator issues. Our widget will automatically detect which package serves which operator and display the pricing options to your customer.

Rapid transfers delivered in just 5 Seconds. Transactions reported directly to your dashboard.

Mobile top-up transfers made with our widget are delivered in just 5 seconds. After the transfer is complete, your customer will receive an option to print a receipt. On your Reloadly dashboard, you’ll have access to reports detailing all transactions made through the widget.
