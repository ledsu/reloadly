The Rise of Payments as a Platform: Trends and Applications in Digital Mobile Payments (Free Report, 2021)

In this special report, Reloadly uncovers the top trends and business applications that are influencing digital mobile payments as we move into 2021.

In so many ways, the Covid-19 pandemic turbo-charged trends in technology that were already beginning to pick up speed throughout the past decade. Among these trends is the rapid adoption of new digital payment methods made possible through emerging PaaP solutions.

This 28-page whitepaper was produced to deliver to you the catalysts that are transforming the payments industry today. Each trend and business application that we cover is backed by current sources and accompanied by rich data in the forms of charts and graphs.

Some insights that we have uncovered in this report include:

  • ⦿ 74% of shoppers who switched to online shopping during the pandemic intend to continue after the pandemic ends.
  • ⦿ In 2020, more people bought cryptocurrencies across the globe than any year before.
  • ⦿ In 2019, the payments sector witnessed a historic influx of investment, attracting $15 billion, 20% more than the preceding year.
  • ⦿ By 2019, mobile money providers had penetrated seven times the reach of ATMs and 20 times that of banks.
